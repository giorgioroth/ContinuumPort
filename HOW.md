# HOW

Most systems try to correct drift.

We prevent it.

---

Not by adding intelligence.

Not by adding memory.

By limiting what is allowed to become real.

---

Before anything exists:

it is validated
it is constrained
it is checked against prior decisions

---

If it does not hold:

it does not happen.

---

State does not move
unless reality proves it.

---

No partial truth.

No silent failure.

No accumulated drift.

---

AI can propose.

It cannot partially change reality.

---

That boundary changes everything.
