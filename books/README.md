# Books

The long-form writing of the ContinuumPort project, gathered in one place. Each book is free to read on GitHub; the source files live at the repository root.

## AI Architectural Thinking
*A Structural Framework for Persistence, Governance, and Continuity*

The conceptual framework behind the project. Parts I and II build the structural model; Part III opens the trust-boundary question — not whether a transition is valid, but on what basis a system is entitled to claim that it is.

[Read on GitHub](https://github.com/giorgioroth/ContinuumPort/blob/main/AI_Architectural_Thinking.md) | [On Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0GS79H49R)

## Seven Principles
*A Discipline for Working Intelligently with AI*

The framework's commitments turned into operating discipline — seven principles for working with persistent AI, written for use rather than proof.

[Read on GitHub](https://github.com/giorgioroth/ContinuumPort/blob/main/The_Seven_Principles.md) | [On Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0H58FW4SG)

## Mechanics of the Algorithm
*The Algorithm and the Human — Code. Culture. Biology. Three clocks. One crisis.*

Five chapters on why the modern crisis is less a failure of technology than a problem of speed — the widening gap between how fast systems move and how slowly human judgment forms. The mechanisms are old; only the rate is new.

[Read on GitHub](https://github.com/giorgioroth/ContinuumPort/blob/main/Mechanics_of_the_Algorithm.md) | [On Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0H6C9RJ48)

## The Discipline of Thinking
*From Observation to Conclusion*

A book about the gap between what a mind notices and what it decides — built one transition at a time: observation into interpretation, evidence into a conclusion no bigger than it can carry. Each chapter hands over a tool before naming it, then says plainly where the tool came from and what is left to claim once the borrowing is subtracted. Nine chapters, from the first collapse to the last question: when all the checking is done, who is left holding the judgment, and what authorizes them to hold it. 

[Read on GitHub](https://github.com/giorgioroth/ContinuumPort/blob/main/The_Discipline_of_Thinking.md) | [On Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/dp/B0H7S2G3CJ)
