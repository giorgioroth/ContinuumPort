## Project Status

ContinuumPort is an exploratory, non-commercial research project.

- Developed by an independent researcher.
- No commercial deployment, users, or customers.
- No revenue generation.
- No large-scale processing of third-party personal data.

All materials are provided as-is, for research, discussion, and exploratory use.
