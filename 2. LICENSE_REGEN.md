The Regen Engine is provided for **evaluation, research, and non-safety-critical development only**.

It is **not certified, not independently safety-validated, and is not represented as fit** for use in safety-critical, high-risk, or life-affecting systems — including but not limited to medical devices, clinical or neural-interface systems, autonomous vehicles, aviation, launch or propulsion control, and industrial safety systems — or any system in which a malfunction could cause death, personal injury, or significant property or environmental harm.

The Regen Engine enforces **only the geometry declared by the integrator**. An admissible execution is not necessarily a safe execution. It does not validate that the declared geometry corresponds to the real world, and it does not determine whether an admissible execution is safe. Defining a correct and complete geometry, validating it independently, and satisfying any applicable regulatory conformity requirements are **solely the integrator's responsibility**.

Any use in the domains named above requires both (a) a separate written agreement with the Author and (b) independent safety qualification and regulatory conformity assessment performed by the integrator. Absent both, such use is unauthorized and entirely at the user's own risk.
